This is our final project, we wanted to program a game based on the gambling game blackjack but with a twist ofc.
Drawing content inspiration from games like balatro and visual inspiration from games like undertale and 2-bit
we made a boss encounter where to win you need to beat your  enemy in 5 hands of blackjack. The twist is that  we added the mechanic of special cards to the game.
Through  which the player can spend their hard earned chips to buy cards that essentially allow them to cheat their way  to victory!
ENJOY PLAYING!!
